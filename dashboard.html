🚀 Getting Started — 3 Steps to Go Live

Complete these steps and fans can start booking you today.

1️⃣
Add your photo & bio below
Scroll down to My Profile — upload a photo and write a short bio so fans know who you are. Then click Save Profile.
2️⃣
Connect your Stripe account to get paid
Scroll down to Earnings and click Connect Stripe. This takes about 5 minutes and lets you receive your 85% cut automatically after every session.
3️⃣
Go live & start earning
Once your profile is set and Stripe is connected, flip the Availability toggle above to Online. Fans can now book Truth ($15) or Dare ($45) sessions with you instantly.
