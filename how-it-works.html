Truth or Dare For My Fans

How It Works

Live, interactive Truth or Dare sessions between fans and their favourite creators — booked in minutes, paid securely, and streamed live.

1

Create your free fan account

Sign up in seconds — just your name, email, and a password. No credit card required to join. Your account lets you track bookings, save favourite creators, and receive session room links.

2

Browse creators and pick your session

Explore creator profiles on the homepage. Each creator offers two session types — choose the one that excites you most.

🤔 Truth — $15  ·  5 minutes  🔥 Dare — $45  ·  15 minutes
3

Submit a booking request

Pick your preferred date and time, add an optional note for the creator, and hit Send Booking Request. No payment is taken at this stage — you only pay once the creator confirms.

4

Creator accepts — complete payment

Once the creator accepts your booking, you'll receive an email with a secure payment link. Click it, review the session details, and complete payment via Stripe. Your spot is locked in.

5

Join your live session

At your session time, click the room link in your email (or from your fan dashboard). Allow camera and microphone access, and you're live with your creator. The session timer starts automatically.

1

Sign up free and get your profile instantly

Register on the homepage — your public creator profile is live immediately at truthordareformyfans.com/creator/yourhandle. Add your photo, bio, and social links to attract fans.

2

Connect Stripe to receive payouts

From your dashboard, click Connect Stripe. This creates a free Stripe Express account linked to your bank. Setup takes about 5 minutes. You'll receive 85% of every session fee paid directly to your account.

3

Go live and start accepting bookings

Flip the Availability toggle in your dashboard to Online. Fans can now see you and submit booking requests. You'll receive an email notification for every new request.

4

Manage bookings from your dashboard

Your Booking Inbox shows all pending requests. For each one, you can Accept & Send Payment Link, Propose a New Time, or Decline. You're always in control of your schedule.

5

Run the session and get paid automatically

Once the fan pays, both of you receive a private video room link. Join at the scheduled time, run your Truth or Dare session, and your 85% payout is processed automatically by Stripe.
